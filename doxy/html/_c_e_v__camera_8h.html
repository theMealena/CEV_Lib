◆ S_CameraMode

enum S_CameraMode

Camera mode behaviour.

note : enable to show more or less field of view before point to follow.

Valeurs énumérées
CAMERA_MED 

follow point is kept centered

CAMERA_THIRD 

follow point is kept at 1/3

CAMERA_QUART 

follow point is kept at 1/4
